Elestial Sound

Elestial Sound is a constantly mutating entity. It is part record label, part live performance troupe, and part art collective. Elestial Sound’s original intention was to be a small label seeking to put out the collected musical efforts of a tight-knit circle of longtime friends, but after nearly five years and fifteen releases, the vision expanded from documenting a musical lineage, to curating an immersive experiential environment.

The logo mark reflects the morphing identity through its modularity and flexibility. By running the mark through a xerox copier, I was able to iterate hundreds of unique marks, each with its own character and unique persona.

Identity and collateral, 2015
